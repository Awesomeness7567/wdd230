California Coast

Coronado Beach is a beautiful beach located in Coronado, California. The beach is situated next to the iconic Hotel del Coronado, which is a popular tourist attraction. The beach is wide and sandy, with gentle surf and picturesque views of the ocean. It is a popular spot for swimming, sunbathing, and building sandcastles. The beach is also dog-friendly, so you can bring your furry friend along.
